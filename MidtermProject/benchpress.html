The Bench Press

Intermediate

Description

Bench press is a popular strength training exercise that targets the chest, shoulders, and triceps. It involves lying on a bench with your feet firmly planted on the floor and a weighted barbell held over your chest. You then lower the barbell towards your chest by bending your elbows, and then push it back up to the starting position. Bench press can be performed with varying levels of weight and is commonly used to build upper body strength, improve posture, and increase overall fitness.
